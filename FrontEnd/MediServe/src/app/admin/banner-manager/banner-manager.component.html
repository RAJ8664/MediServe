How to Use Cloudinary Images

  1. Upload your image to your Cloudinary account
  2. Copy the secure URL of the image from your Cloudinary dashboard
  3. Paste the URL in the input field above
  4. Click "Update Banner" to apply the new image

Note: For optimal performance, it's recommended to use optimized images with dimensions of at least 800x600 pixels.
